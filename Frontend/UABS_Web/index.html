Universal Appointment Booking System (UABS)

A unified platform booking and managing appointments.

Welcome

Use this portal to book appointments, manage services, and view analytic reports.

UABS supports four types of accounts:

Customer: book and manage appointments.

Business: offer services, manage availability and bookings.

Data Broker: access aggregated business reports (if approved).

Admin: manage accounts, verifications, and data access.
